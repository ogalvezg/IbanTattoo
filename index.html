About Me

I'm a tattoo artist based at Red Chapel Tattoo Studio in Berlin. My main focus is figurative related subjects often using a reduced colour palette. I like to pay special attention to the way the tattoo fits and flows with the form of the body, whilst I also follow the fundamentals of the old school tattoo look. "Bold will Hold"
